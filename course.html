Actuarial & Applied Mathematics

A four year program that provides an all rounder courses and training for students who intend to pursue the carreer of actuarial. This program gives students a wide and depth exposure to mathematics, business, finance, and science, from theoretical to applied. Graduates of this program will be equipped with mathematical ability and critical thinking skill to be globally competitive. This program gives students an attractive option for those who are interested in pursuing advanced degrees in mathematical or quantitative finance and master's degrees in business administration, as well as in securing employment in the banking and insurance industries.

Communication Science

This program will prepare students for further studies and career in speech-language pathology and audiology. This coursework includes study in communication sciences and disorders, psychology, English/linguistics, as well as statistics, dentistry, and research design. The undergraduate coursework in this program emphasizes the basic processes and acquisition of normal communication in children and adults. Bachelor of this program will assist individuals with communication impairments with various techniques, methods, and treatments. Helping children and adults to overcome difficulties in hearing, speech, language and learning.

Computer Science

Cyber Security

A bachelor's degree in this program puts students at the forefront of system and data protetction. This program designs a combination of courseworks from computer science, information systems, and mathematics into a dynamic program that brings students to solve modern problems. Students will develop knowledge in cryptography, cloud security, incident response, risk management, digital forensics, ethical hacking, and penetration testing. Students will learn computer security from both a software and hardware perspective focusing on building and maintaining more secure systems. Students will be able to take roles in either public or private sectors.
