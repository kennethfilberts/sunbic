About Us

Sunbic University was established in 1998 and it is located in the center of Jakarta. Sunbic University is well-known for its advanced facilities and technologies as well as good courses. Sunbic University has 6 majors focusing in communication and technology. With online courses available for the students, Sunbic University has the ideal teaching and learning opportunities for each and every student. Sunbic University has received high rankings in reviews and topped the national rankings. Sunbic University also are proud to introduce many of outstanding graduates and cooperating companies.

Vision

To become a world-class campus that plays an active role in the development of science and information technology-based communication.

Mission

1. Organize and develop international standard education

2. Develop and disseminate internationally recognized science, technology, and information technology-based communication

3. Utilizing science, technology, management, and communication based on information technology, for the welfareand progress of Indonesia
